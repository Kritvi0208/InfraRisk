# Credit Risk Modelling

## Probability of Default Analysis

Using Monte Carlo simulation on 500+ projects:
- P50 PD: 2.8%
- P90 PD: 8.5%
- Correlation with DSCR: -0.92

## Loss Given Default

- Avg LGD: 28%
- Range: 15-45% (sector dependent)
- Recovery mechanisms: restructuring (70%), asset sale (20%), insurance (10%)
